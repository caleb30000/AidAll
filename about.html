What is AidAll?

AidAll is an independent platform helping to connect the needy with potential donors.

This website is a public bulletin. We encourage everyone with spare properties to post a listing and to mark their listing as filled once they have successfully donated.

Why did I make this website?

I made AidAll so that needy people could get in contact with potential donors as fast as possible.

What information do you need?

For the needy, asks for your nearest city to display the closest listings. We do not track your precise location.

We provide the needy with a guide of some safety notes for contacting donors. The needy are ultimately responsible for their own safety.
